Palm Harbor Mullius – 1,409 Sq. Ft. of Flexible Living

The Palm Harbor Mullius stands out as one of the most versatile homes in The Home Boys’ lineup. At 1,409 square feet, this single-section home can be configured as a two-bedroom with a den, or as a true three-bedroom, offering flexibility for downsizers, retirees, or growing families.

Key Features:

  • Layouts: Available in 2-bed/1-bath, 2-bed/2-bath, or 3-bed/2-bath.
  • Porches: Three options—prow front porch, serenity package, or a classic straight porch.
  • Kitchen Designs: Choose between a curved peninsula layout or an open-concept island kitchen.
  • Décor Packages: Four professionally curated schemes (Yellowstone, Cascadia, Cove Creek, Seabrook) let you personalize cabinets, counters, tile, and finishes.

Interior Highlights:

Inside, the Mullius features vaulted ceilings soaring up to 10 feet, architectural windows, and abundant natural light. Knotty alder cabinetry, stainless steel appliances, tile flooring, and a stainless farm sink bring modern function with rustic charm. A flexible den can serve as an office, studio, or an additional bedroom. The primary suite includes dual closets and an en-suite with tile shower, dual sinks, and storage.

Built for the Northwest:

As an Energy Star and NEEM-certified home, the Mullius offers superior insulation and a whole-home ventilation system for year-round comfort. Built to HUD code, it’s designed to perform efficiently in the Northwest climate.
